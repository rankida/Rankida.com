THE RANKIDA APPROACH

A simple path to success

Rankida is a unashamedly young and small company. Based in Glasgow, Scotland, we strongly beleive it is our youth and size that allows us to respond quickly to our customer's needs. Change is part of projects, and rather than try to eliminate it we embrace it. Unlike many large corporations, we want to get to know you, your company and what you are trying to acheive.

Primarily our projects are centred around your goals. We look past user requirements to focus on what the business and users need to acheive success. To keep orientated, we encourage an itterative approach. Small, regular itterations demonstrate progress and allow all stakeholders to ensure the project is moving in the right direction. We provide regular demos, and where possible a working development system, to foster continual feedback from all quarters.

Our delivered solutions are built using the latest in web and mobile technologies. Building apone technologies like Node.js, React, Xamarin, C# .Net and many many more allow us to acheive cutting-edge solutions with minimum investment. At Rankida we fully embrace the cloud. Be it migrating an existing on-premise website or managing a new cloud deployment, the cloud allows you to focus on what your company does best while Rankida keeps everything running smoothly.
